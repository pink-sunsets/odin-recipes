Lemon Chicken

Lemon Chicken is a wonderful twist on a boilerplate chicken that amps up the flavour for little effort

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Lemon
  • Cornstarch
  • Soy sauce
  • Water or, optionally, Stock (meat or vegetable)
  • Salt Pepper, Oil

Preparation

  1. Slice the chicken in strips
  2. Cook it on high heat just to get some browning
  3. In a bowl, mix the water/stock, lemon juice, soy sauce, cornstarch, salt, pepper ad oil
  4. Briefly cook the sauce until it starts to thicken
  5. Mix the chicken strips back in
  6. When the sauce is thick enough the chicken will be cooked through
  7. Dont forget to season the final plate

Enjoy!
