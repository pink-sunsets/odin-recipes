Avocado Toast

The Avocado Toast is a quick dish packed with taste, healthy fats and proteins

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Avocado
  • Toppings: Eggs, Salmon, Fresh cheese, Nuts, or whatever you come up with!
  • Salt, Pepper, Lemon

Preparation

  1. Slice and toast the bread
  2. Prepare the avocado, either in slices or diced. If you want, you can season it
  3. If needed, cook and prepare the toppings
  4. Assemble your toast
  5. Dont forget to season it

Enjoy!
